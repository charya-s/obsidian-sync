### Two Twos

Both **二** and **两** can be used for the number “2”. 

> **二** is used for “two”, “twelve”, “twenty”, Tuesday, February, etc.
> **两** is used for “two hundred”, 


Examples:
- wǒ shì Charya / 我是 Charya → I am Charya.
- nǐ hěn piàoliang / 你很漂亮 → You very pretty.
