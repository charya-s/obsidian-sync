### New Words

zài = 在 -> at/in/exists
nâr = 那 -> where
nâlî = 哪里 -> where

---
### Where is X?

To ask where a subject is, you can use:

> **subject + 在 + 那/哪里**
> *subject at/in/exists where?*


Take note that **那** is more common in the North (Beijing), while **哪里** is more common in the South (Shanghai).
