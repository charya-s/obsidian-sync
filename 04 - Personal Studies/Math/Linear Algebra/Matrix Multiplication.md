This is the basic algorithm at the heart of linear algebra. 

$$
\begin{align}

A \cdot B = \begin{bmatrix}
x_{1} & x_{2} & x_{3} \\
x_{4} & x_{5} & x_{6}
\end{bmatrix}
\begin{bmatrix}
y_{1} & y_{2} \\
y_{3} & y_{4} \\
y_{5} & y_{6}
\end{bmatrix}
\\ =
\begin{bmatrix}
x_{1}y_{1}+x_{2}y_{3}+x_{3}y_{5} \\
x_{3} & _{4}
\end{bmatrix}

\end{align}
$$
